APPLICATION PROCESS

At Wave Financial, our application process is designed to be straightforward and user-friendly.
Follow these simple steps to get started:

01

APPLICATION

Your CV arrives. We review applicants based on various criteria to ensure the best fit for our team.


02

SCREENING

HR interview - you will meet a member of the Talent Acquisition team (~30 min). Technical testing (20 min - 1 h) to assess your skills.

03

INTERVIEWING + TECHNICAL ASSESSMENT
You will meet the technical lead of your team (~1 h). For some positions, we combine the HR and Technical interview.

4

JOB OFFER

Brace yourself and enjoy the ride. Expect our offer via email once the selection process is completed.
