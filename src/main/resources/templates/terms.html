3. What is a cookie?

Cookies are small files with information that the internet website sends to the visitor’s browser. The browser stores this information in a file on the end user device – that means your computer, mobile phone, tablet or other smart device. Cookies allow visitors to navigate the website and, when applicable, enable the Bank to personalise the website and its content to the needs of the visitor as well as to improve the results of their actions. The cookies are also used to memorize your preferences when searching information on our website, to personalize information shown, to count website visitors, to facilitate user’s registration for services offered via the website and for the protection of user’s data.
